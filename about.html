about me

Hi, I’m Hugo. I’m a creative technologist with a passion for questioning how we interact with machines. I build my own softwares and experimental interfaces to challenge the defaults. I believe that technology should be designed keeping agency and transparency in mind, while echoing the technical reality underneath.

I studied cinematography and game design in Montpellier before completing my master's degree in media design at the Saint-Étienne design school (ESADSE). I'm currently working as a graphic programmer / interaction designer at m-box, a Berlin based design studio for interactive and immersive installations.
