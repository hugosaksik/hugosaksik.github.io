Systématiser, Vera Molnár

DNSEP Part 2

Systématiser is the diploma I presented in June 2021 at the ESADSE, as well as the state of the art of a research project in progress.

The methodology of work is based on the one recommended by the CRAV (Centre de Recherche en Arts Visuels), created in 1960. In opposition of the "genius artist" producing artworks ex nihilo, the CRAV suggest an artistic practice inspired by science, based on a series of visual experimentations.

Systématiser takes as a starting point the artworks of two artists who belonged to the CRAV: the series Hommage à Dürer by Vera Molnár, and Lumières alternées by Julio Le Parc.

In a process close to reverse engineering, algorithms reproducing the shapes of the original Molnár's artwork are written on Processing. Then follows a series of variations of the graphic properties of the artwork: overlay of lines, dots contained in specific shapes, random distribution of coordinates ...

As we move forward in the iterations, the experimentations drift from printed paper to a graphical generation digital tool including properties missing from the reference work: interactions, distortions developed in time, tridimension.

If the original desire of the project is to reveal the beauty that lies beneath the instructions or properties of the original artworks, it is also an attempt at showing how contemporary creation could transmit a culture of the digital art history.
